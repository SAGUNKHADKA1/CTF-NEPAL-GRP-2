What is Engage Nepal with Science?

Engage Nepal with Science is a research scientist teams that helps to deliever workshop inorder to develop a culture of engage with science.

What is the aim of Engage Nepal with Science?

The main aim of Engage Nepal with Science is to inspire,empower and build confidence in Science-Technology-Engineering-Mathematics.

What is the role of Engage Nepal with Science?

The role of Engage Nepal with Science is to work with different schools to improve student's learning more creative,interactive and helps to boost their engagement and science communication skills at their understanding level.

Who are involved in Engage Nepal with Science?

This project is developed by Alba Abad researcher of WCB who are collaborate between Research Institute for BioScience and Biotechnology (RIBB,Nepal)and WCB.
